Checkpoints in Blackbird workflows are control steps that enable Birds to pause and wait for different events before continuing their Flights. These triggers could be a mix of automated and human-driven actions. Instead of following a single, linear trigger path, Checkpoints allow for flexible handling of workflows where pauses for approvals, reviews, or external events are necessary.
